QUESTION 2: Explain why Bridged mode gives malware a map to your local network.
Bridged mode assigns the sandbox a direct IP address on the host's physical subnet. This allows malware to bypass the host OS, enumerate local network devices, and execute lateral movement attacks against adjacent hardware like routers or personal computers.
