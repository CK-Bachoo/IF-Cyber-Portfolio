
Group Policy Audit Report - Session 14



1. What is the processing order of Group Policy?

Answer: Local, Site, Domain, Organizational Unit (LSDOU).



2. If a domain policy conflicts with an OU policy, which wins?

Answer: The OU policy wins because it is applied last in the LSDOU sequence, overwriting previous settings.



3. What does gpupdate /force do?

Answer: It compels an immediate update of all Group Policy settings across the system instead of waiting for the standard background refresh cycle.
